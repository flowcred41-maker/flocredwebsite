FAQ

Questions about ARIA & FLOCRED

What is ARIA and how is she different from a regular form?

ARIA (AI Responsive Intelligence Advisor) is India's first AI finance concierge. Unlike a form that gives yes/no, ARIA analyses your complete profile and gives you a plan: which lender, at what rate, with exactly which documents. She builds a CIBIL roadmap if needed and alerts you when rates change.

Does chatting with ARIA affect my CIBIL score?

No. ARIA uses a soft inquiry that does not appear on your CIBIL report and has zero score impact. Only when you formally apply to a lender does their system run a hard enquiry. ARIA minimises unnecessary hard enquiries by matching you to lenders most likely to approve.

Can ARIA speak Hindi?

Yes. Chat in Hindi, Hinglish, or English — ARIA responds in whatever language you use. Just ask naturally and she'll explain everything clearly without financial jargon.

What is the Holiday in EMI product?

Holiday in EMI is FLOCRED's exclusive product — a built-in moratorium where you get your loan disbursed and pay ₹0 EMI for 3–6 months. Available on personal, home, and business loans through our partner lenders. Learn more →

Is FLOCRED a bank? Do you lend directly?

No. FLOCRED Private Limited (CIN: U67100DL2022PTC400764) is a Lending Service Provider (LSP) under RBI's Digital Lending Guidelines (September 2022). We connect you with regulated banks and NBFCs. All credit decisions are made by those institutions — we never lend directly.

How does FLOCRED make money if it's free for borrowers?

FLOCRED receives a referral fee from the lender when a loan is disbursed through our platform. This does not create additional cost for the borrower — the rate you get is the same whether you go direct to the bank or via FLOCRED.
