❤️ Section 8 Non-Profit

FLOCRED Micro Foundation

We believe fair credit is a right, not a privilege. FLOCRED Micro Foundation runs financial literacy programs for underserved communities across Delhi NCR — free of charge.

📚

Financial Literacy Camps

Free workshops in Dwarka, Rohini, Faridabad, and Okhla. Teaching CIBIL basics, loan awareness, and predatory lending red flags.

🧠

ARIA for All

Free ARIA access for borrowers earning below ₹25,000/month — India's underserved credit segment.

👩‍💼

Women's Finance Initiative

Dedicated loan matching and financial coaching for women entrepreneurs in Delhi NCR. No fees. No conditions.

📈

Credit Builder Program

Helping thin-file borrowers build credit history through structured microfinance and secured credit products.
