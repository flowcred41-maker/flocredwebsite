FAQ

Personal loan questions answered

What CIBIL score do I need for a business loan?

Most banks require 700+. ICICI Bank approves from 680+. Bajaj Finserv and HDB Financial approve from 660+ but at higher rates (13–16%). If your score is below 650, ARIA will recommend NBFCs and build you a 60-day score improvement plan.

What is the maximum business loan I can get in Delhi?

ICICI Bank and HDFC offer up to ₹2 Crore. SBI offers up to ₹20 Lakh. The actual amount depends on your income, existing EMIs, and CIBIL score. ARIA calculates your exact maximum in 2 minutes.

How fast is business loan disbursal in Delhi NCR?

HDFC and ICICI disburse in 24–48 hours for salaried employees with complete documents. Bajaj Finserv in 24 hours. Government bank SBI takes 3–5 working days. ARIA can tell you which bank is fastest for your specific profile.

Does checking eligibility on FLOCRED affect my CIBIL?

No. FLOCRED uses a soft inquiry that does not appear on your CIBIL report. Only when you formally apply to a bank does their system run a hard enquiry. ARIA minimises unnecessary hard enquiries by matching you to the lender most likely to approve.

Can I get a business loan with a low CIBIL score in Delhi?

Yes. Bajaj Finserv (660+), HDB Financial (600+), and Tata Capital (680+) approve lower CIBIL profiles at 13–16%. ARIA also builds you a personalised score improvement plan — most borrowers see 40–60 point improvement in 60–90 days.
