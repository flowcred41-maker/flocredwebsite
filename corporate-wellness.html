FAQ

Corporate wellness questions

Is this really free for our company?

Yes, 100% free. FLOCRED earns commission from banks when an employee takes a loan through ARIA. This fully funds the service. Your company pays nothing, and employees pay nothing for ARIA access.

Is employee financial data private?

Absolutely. Individual employee data is never shared with HR or the employer. The HR dashboard shows only aggregate, anonymised wellness indicators. FLOCRED complies with DPDPA 2023 for all employee data.

Do you need IT integration or system access?

No. The entire setup is via WhatsApp and email — no API integration, no CRM access, no IT involvement. We just need your employee email list or WhatsApp numbers to onboard.

What size companies do you work with?

Any size — from 10-person startups to 1,000+ employee corporations. All get the same ARIA access. Larger companies get dedicated account management and customised financial literacy sessions.
