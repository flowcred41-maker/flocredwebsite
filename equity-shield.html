FAQ

Equity Shield questions

Is Equity Shield really free?

Yes, completely free for all loans sourced through FLOCRED. There is no monthly fee, no enrolment fee, and no hidden charges. FLOCRED's business model is commission from lenders — your protection costs you nothing.

What if my loan is from a bank I didn't get through FLOCRED?

Equity Shield's full monitoring is available for FLOCRED-sourced loans. If you have an existing loan elsewhere, our team can still help with restructuring advice and lender negotiations as a paid service. Contact us to discuss.

How does ARIA monitor my EMI health?

ARIA uses the profile information you shared at loan origination — income, employment, existing EMIs — and cross-references it with your self-reported updates. She doesn't access your bank account directly. The monitoring is based on your financial profile and payment pattern signals.

What counts as an NPA?

An account is classified as a Non-Performing Asset (NPA) when EMI payments are overdue for 90+ days. Equity Shield's early warning triggers at 30 days of risk detection — well before the 90-day NPA threshold.
