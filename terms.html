Rates marked with * are starting rates for best-profile applicants. Actual rates may be higher. Processing fees, prepayment charges, and other costs vary by lender and are disclosed by the Lender directly.

FLOCRED has referral arrangements with partner banks and NBFCs. When a loan is disbursed through FLOCRED, we receive a referral fee from the Lender. This does not result in additional cost to the borrower. FLOCRED's service is always free to borrowers.

About FLOCRED Micro Foundation

FLOCRED Micro Foundation is a not-for-profit organisation established under the Companies Act 2013 (Section 8). Our mission is to promote financial literacy among underserved communities in Delhi NCR — particularly daily wage workers, informal sector employees, and first-generation borrowers who lack access to fair credit.

Our Programs

  • Financial Literacy Camps: Free workshops in Dwarka, Rohini, Faridabad, and Okhla teaching CIBIL score basics and loan awareness
  • ARIA for All: Free access to ARIA's loan guidance for individuals below ₹25,000/month income
  • Credit Builder Program: Helping thin-file borrowers establish credit history through secured credit cards and microfinance
  • Women's Finance Initiative: Dedicated loan matching and financial coaching for women entrepreneurs in Delhi NCR
